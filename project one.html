I was tasked to conduct research on a specific subculture (in my case Wing Chun Sifus). After starting with desk research and formulating curiosities I created research questions that would guide me through the process. The first research technique I used was observation. I contacted the owner of a local Wing Chun school and arranged a trial lesson where I could observe and participate. Although I had just arrived in The Hague and was unfamiliar with my surroundings, I managed to arrange two interviews with Wing Chun Sifus. Both interviews were very insightful and extensive. The second interview I conducted was 5h long in total. I was able to really emphasise, concentrate and ask the right questions even over such a long time. From the recordings I transcribed both interviews and made affinity diagrams combining the findings. I used the diagram to identify clusters and connections. Then I translated the data gathered into relative insights. Lastly, I used these insights to formulate HMW questions which I translated into design directions. This concluded the first part of the course.
